COMPONENTS-
*arduino uno
*bluetooth module
*android app
*connecting wires
*relay
PROCEDURE-
*open arduino software and upload this code on arduino.
*connect proper terminals of bluetooth module to arduino.  
#note- never connect bluetooth module first and then apply code.
#make sure that Tx and Rx terminals of bluetooth is connected to
 Rx and Tx terminal respectively. same terminal should not be 
 connected to each other.
*use relay to connect arduino and electrical appliance , refer relay 
 circuit diagram for connections.
*download any bluetooth android voice controlled app to give commands.
 eg-"Bluetooth arduino voice command"
*connect your mobile and arduino over bluetooth connection.
